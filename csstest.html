Scratch, the Savior

For the unknowing, let me explain.
Scratch is a website dedicated to teaching younger kids the basics of computer science and programming. The Scratch editor is a multipurpose editor, providing a graphics editor, sound editor, and program editor all in one. This environment lets kids just screw around and do whatever they wanted to (within reason, of course)
I started using Scratch in 2015, messing around with it very occasionaly. In May of 2016, I made my first scratch account

And thus, my treacherous journey in programming started.
